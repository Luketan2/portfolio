About me

luke.tan04@gmail.com  ·  0408 714 149

I'm a final-year Robotics & Mechatronics Engineering student at Monash University (AI stream), graduating in 2026 with a High Distinction average. I'm currently Science Lead on Monash Nova Rover, where I design and build science payloads for international rover competitions.

I've been building things since I was a kid — RC planes, bouldering walls, bed frames — and that maker instinct has carried into my engineering work. I care about systems that actually function under real conditions: dusty desert environments, tight competition timelines, and constrained budgets.

My technical focus is at the intersection of embedded systems, mechanical design, and robotics. I'm comfortable across the full stack — from Fusion 360 CAD through to firmware on ESP32 and STM32, CAN bus integration, and physical fabrication.
